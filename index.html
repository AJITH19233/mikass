About Mikasa Doors

When we think of a door, we think of wood and the craftsman who would create it. In a market served by traditional flush doors, Mikasa doors and frames introduces a revolutionary concept. It offers factory finished, ready to use door sets (Door + Frame) to suit every room and purpose. All the components – door leaf, frame and architectural ironmongery – work together as a unified product, meeting certified performance levels.
